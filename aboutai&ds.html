What is Ai and Data science ?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. It includes areas like machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics.
Data Science is the study of data to extract useful insights using statistics, programming, and analysis. It helps in making decisions based on patterns and trends found in data.
AI and Data Science work together — data science provides the data and analysis, while AI uses that data to learn and act intelligently. Both fields are widely used in healthcare, finance, education, and technology today.
